About Us

MangyshNet is an innovative initiative aimed at providing sustainable internet access to every corner of the Mangystau region.

We strive to overcome the challenges of unstable connectivity and lack of internet in rural and small urban areas to improve quality of life and expand opportunities in education, healthcare, business, and government digital services.

MangyshNet is an ambitious project with the goal of creating a hybrid internet infrastructure that combines:

  • Satellite communication stations
  • Fiber optic backbones
  • LTE/5G repeaters
  • Local mesh networks
  • Wi-Fi hubs in settlements

Our Mission

  • Solving the problem of unstable connectivity and lack of internet in remote areas.
  • Increasing access to online education, telemedicine, government services, internet banking, and digital commerce.
  • Creating conditions for the development of local communities and economic activity.

Join us in this important and promising project — together we will bring the digital future closer to every resident of the Mangystau region.
