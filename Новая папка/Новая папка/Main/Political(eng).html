Privacy Policy

This privacy policy defines how MangyshNet collects, uses, and protects information provided by users when using our website and services.

1. Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, surname, and contact details (phone number, email, etc.);
  • Connection information and device technical specifications;
  • Geolocation data (when using the coverage map);
  • Support request history.

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing quality communication services;
  • Feedback and technical support;
  • Improving the service and expanding coverage;
  • Analyzing user requests and statistics.

3. Storage and Protection

All data is protected using modern technical and organizational security measures. Only authorized MangyshNet employees have access to the information.

4. Transfer to Third Parties

We do not transfer your personal data to third parties without your consent, except as required by the laws of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies to improve the website and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. User Rights

You have the right to request deletion or modification of your personal data by contacting our support service.
